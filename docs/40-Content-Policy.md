# 40 - Content Policy

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** Production Ready\
**Document Type:** Content Policy

**Effective Date:** To be announced

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Purpose

This Content Policy explains what content may be published on Ravenshaw
Moments, how content is reviewed, and the actions that may be taken when
content violates platform rules.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to all content published on the Platform, including:

-   User profiles
-   Photos
-   Videos
-   Gallery uploads
-   Event posts
-   Competition submissions
-   News
-   Announcements
-   Organization pages
-   Department pages
-   Hostel pages
-   Comments (future)
-   Contribution information

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Guiding Principles

Content should be:

-   Accurate
-   Respectful
-   Lawful
-   Relevant
-   Authentic
-   Educational or community-focused

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. User-Generated Content

Users remain responsible for all content they upload.

By submitting content, users confirm that:

-   They own the content or have permission to share it.
-   The content does not violate applicable laws.
-   The content does not infringe intellectual property rights.
-   The content complies with this Policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Acceptable Content

Examples include:

-   Campus memories
-   Academic achievements
-   Event photographs
-   Competition updates
-   Alumni stories
-   Historical archives
-   Educational resources
-   Volunteer activities
-   Department and hostel updates

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Prohibited Content

The following content is prohibited:

-   Hate speech
-   Harassment
-   Threats
-   Defamation
-   Obscene or sexually explicit material
-   Graphic violence
-   Terrorism or extremist content
-   Fraudulent information
-   Spam
-   Malware or phishing links
-   Impersonation
-   Copyright infringement
-   Illegal activities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. AI-Generated Content

AI-assisted content is permitted when:

-   It is not deceptive.
-   It does not impersonate real people.
-   It complies with copyright laws.
-   It follows all other platform policies.

Administrators may label or remove misleading AI-generated content.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Photos & Videos

Uploads should:

-   Respect privacy.
-   Avoid exposing sensitive personal information.
-   Be appropriate for a university community.
-   Comply with copyright requirements.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. News & Announcements

News published on the Platform should:

-   Be factual.
-   Identify sources where appropriate.
-   Avoid misleading headlines.
-   Distinguish opinion from fact.

The Platform is not an official source of university announcements.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Competitions & Certificates

Competition submissions must be original unless rules permit otherwise.

Participation certificates remain private.

Winner certificates may be displayed publicly after official approval.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Content Review

Content may be:

-   Automatically screened
-   Reviewed by moderators
-   Escalated to Super Admin
-   Removed when necessary

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Reporting Violations

Users may report content that appears to violate this Policy.

All reports are reviewed according to the Moderation Policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

-   Content removal
-   Warning
-   Temporary restrictions
-   Account suspension
-   Permanent account termination

Serious or unlawful content may be reported to appropriate authorities
where legally required.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 14. Appeals

Users may request a review of moderation decisions through the official
appeal process.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 15. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Platform
after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
