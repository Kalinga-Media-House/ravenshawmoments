# 48 - Child Safety Policy

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** Production Ready\
**Document Type:** Child Safety Policy

**Effective Date:** To be announced

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Purpose

Ravenshaw Moments is committed to providing a safe and respectful online
environment. This Policy explains how the Platform helps protect
children and young users from exploitation, abuse, harassment, and
inappropriate content.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Scope

This Policy applies to:

-   User accounts
-   Profiles
-   Photos
-   Videos
-   Events
-   Competitions
-   Messages (future)
-   Comments (future)
-   Media uploads
-   Community interactions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Commitment

The Platform has zero tolerance for:

-   Child sexual abuse or exploitation
-   Grooming
-   Sexualization of minors
-   Child abuse material
-   Human trafficking involving minors
-   Any illegal activity involving children

Any such content or behavior may result in immediate account suspension
and, where legally required, reporting to the appropriate authorities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Minimum Age

Users should meet the minimum age required under applicable law to
create and manage their own account.

Where parental or guardian consent is legally required, users are
responsible for ensuring such consent has been obtained.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Privacy for Young Users

The Platform seeks to protect personal information by:

-   Limiting public exposure of sensitive data
-   Providing privacy controls
-   Restricting administrator access to authorized personnel
-   Following the Privacy Policy

Sensitive information such as phone numbers, email addresses, and roll
numbers is not publicly displayed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Safe Community Standards

Users must not:

-   Harass or threaten children
-   Share exploitative material
-   Encourage dangerous activities
-   Request sensitive personal information from minors
-   Impersonate children

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Content Moderation

The Platform may:

-   Remove harmful content
-   Restrict visibility
-   Suspend offending accounts
-   Review reported material
-   Preserve evidence where legally necessary

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Reporting

Users are encouraged to report any suspected child safety concern
immediately through the Platform's reporting process or official contact
channels.

Reports are reviewed as a priority.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

-   Immediate content removal
-   Account suspension
-   Permanent account termination
-   Restriction of future access
-   Referral to law enforcement where required

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Education

The Platform encourages responsible online behavior and respectful
participation within the university community.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal, regulatory, or
operational changes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
