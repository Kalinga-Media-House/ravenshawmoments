# 45 - Data Retention

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** Production Ready\
**Document Type:** Data Retention Policy

**Effective Date:** To be announced

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Purpose

This Policy explains how Ravenshaw Moments retains, archives, and
securely deletes information throughout its lifecycle. The goal is to
balance user privacy, operational needs, legal compliance, and
preservation of university history.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Guiding Principles

-   Data minimization
-   Purpose limitation
-   Secure storage
-   Privacy by default
-   Lawful retention
-   Controlled deletion
-   Historical preservation where appropriate

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Data Categories

The Platform may retain:

-   User accounts
-   Public profiles
-   Private profile information
-   Competition records
-   Event registrations
-   Certificates
-   Contributions
-   Media uploads
-   Audit logs
-   Security logs
-   Backups

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. User Accounts

Active accounts are retained while the account remains in use.

Users may request account deletion subject to legal, operational, or
security obligations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Deleted Accounts

When deletion is approved:

-   Personal information is removed or anonymized where reasonably
    possible.
-   Public references may be anonymized to preserve historical
    integrity.
-   Records required by law or security investigations may be retained.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Profile Data

Public profile information remains visible according to user privacy
settings.

Private information is accessible only to authorized systems and
administrators.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Certificates

Issued certificates are intended to remain available for long-term
verification.

Revoked certificates are not deleted; they remain marked as revoked in
the verification system.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Competition Records

Competition records may be retained for:

-   Result verification
-   Certificate validation
-   Historical archives
-   Fraud prevention

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Contribution Records

Contribution records may be retained to:

-   Verify payments
-   Prevent fraud
-   Support financial accountability
-   Resolve disputes

Payment screenshots remain private.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Media

Photos and videos remain available until:

-   Removed by the owner (where permitted)
-   Removed through moderation
-   Deleted under legal requirements

Historical archives may be preserved where appropriate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Audit Logs

Administrative and security logs may be retained for operational,
security, and compliance purposes.

Logs should not be modified except through approved archival processes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Backups

Regular backups are maintained to support disaster recovery.

Expired backups are securely removed according to operational schedules.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Legal Hold

If required by law, investigation, or litigation, deletion requests may
be delayed until the legal hold is released.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 14. Secure Deletion

When deletion is appropriate, the Platform aims to:

-   Remove unnecessary personal data
-   Revoke unnecessary access
-   Securely erase storage where practical
-   Prevent unauthorized recovery

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 15. User Rights

Where applicable, users may:

-   Request access
-   Correct inaccurate information
-   Request deletion
-   Manage profile visibility

Some requests may be limited by legal or security obligations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 16. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect operational or legal
changes.
