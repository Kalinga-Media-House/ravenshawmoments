# 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Ravenshaw Moments ("Platform", "we",
"our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or use the
Platform.

By continuing to use the Platform, you acknowledge the use of cookies as
described in this Policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser.
They help websites remember preferences, improve security, and provide a
better user experience.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

-   Maintain secure login sessions
-   Remember user preferences
-   Improve website performance
-   Analyze platform usage
-   Protect against fraud and abuse
-   Provide a consistent experience

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Types of Cookies

## Essential Cookies

Required for core functionality, including:

-   User authentication
-   Session management
-   Security protections
-   Form submission

These cookies cannot be disabled without affecting platform
functionality.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Preference Cookies

Used to remember:

-   Theme (Light/Dark/System)
-   Language (future)
-   User interface preferences

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Analytics Cookies

Used to understand:

-   Page visits
-   Navigation patterns
-   Performance metrics
-   Error reporting

Analytics data is used in aggregate to improve the Platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Functional Cookies

Support enhanced features such as:

-   Remembering recent searches
-   Notification preferences
-   Accessibility settings

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Third-Party Services

The Platform may use trusted third-party providers, such as:

-   Cloudflare
-   Supabase
-   Vercel
-   Analytics providers
-   Email service providers

These services may set their own cookies subject to their respective
privacy policies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to:

-   View stored cookies
-   Delete cookies
-   Block cookies
-   Restrict third-party cookies

Disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the Platform from
functioning correctly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Future Cookie Consent

Future versions of the Platform may provide a cookie consent banner
allowing users to manage non-essential cookie preferences.
