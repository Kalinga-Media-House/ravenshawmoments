# Executive Summary

Ravenshaw Moments is an independent, community-driven digital ecosystem
built for the Ravenshaw University community. It is designed to preserve
memories, celebrate achievements, connect generations, and create a
permanent digital archive of the university's history.

The platform combines social networking, digital archives, competition
management, certificate generation, contributions, alumni engagement,
and university community services into a single scalable platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Objectives

-   Preserve student memories forever.
-   Digitally archive Ravenshaw University's heritage.
-   Connect students, teachers, alumni, organizations, and supporters.
-   Recognize academic, cultural, sports, and community achievements.
-   Provide a secure and privacy-first platform.
-   Build a sustainable ecosystem through voluntary contributions and
    sponsorships.
