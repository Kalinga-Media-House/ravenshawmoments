# 23 - Alumni

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** 🔒 Frozen\
**Document Type:** Alumni System Specification

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

This document defines the official alumni ecosystem for Ravenshaw
Moments, enabling lifelong engagement between former students and the
university community.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Objectives

-   Maintain lifelong alumni profiles.
-   Preserve alumni achievements.
-   Strengthen alumni networking.
-   Encourage mentorship and contributions.
-   Archive the legacy of Ravenshaw graduates.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Eligibility

Eligible users include:

-   Former students
-   Former research scholars
-   Retired faculty (optional future support)

Verification is required before an alumni badge is granted.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Profile

Each profile may include:

-   Name
-   Profile Photo
-   Department
-   Batch
-   Degree
-   Current Occupation
-   Organization / Employer
-   City & Country
-   Biography
-   Social Links (optional)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Directory

Search and filter by:

-   Department
-   Batch
-   Graduation Year
-   Profession
-   Location

Only public information is searchable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Distinguished Alumni

Super Admin may feature notable alumni based on:

-   Public service
-   Academia
-   Business
-   Sports
-   Arts & Culture
-   Research
-   Entrepreneurship
-   Community impact

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Achievements

Profiles may showcase:

-   Awards
-   Publications
-   Startups
-   Professional milestones
-   Public recognition

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Networking

Future capabilities:

-   Alumni messaging
-   Mentorship requests
-   Networking events
-   Career guidance

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Events

Supports:

-   Reunions
-   Department meets
-   Guest lectures
-   Mentorship sessions
-   Fundraising events

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Alumni Contributions

Alumni may:

-   Donate
-   Sponsor competitions
-   Support departments
-   Join the Top Contributors list (after verification)

Contribution history appears on their profile according to privacy
settings.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Success Stories

Featured alumni stories include:

-   Career journey
-   Achievements
-   Interviews
-   Inspirational articles

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Privacy

Public:

-   Name
-   Batch
-   Department
-   Achievements
-   Public contributions (with consent)

Private:

-   Contact details
-   Donation records
-   Sensitive personal information
