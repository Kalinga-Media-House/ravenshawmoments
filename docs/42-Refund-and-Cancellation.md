# 42 - Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** Production Ready\
**Document Type:** Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Effective Date:** To be announced

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Purpose

This Policy explains how refunds, cancellations, and payment-related
requests are handled on Ravenshaw Moments.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Scope

This Policy applies to:

-   Competition registration fees
-   Event registration fees
-   Voluntary contributions
-   Sponsorship payments
-   Future premium services (if introduced)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Competition Registration

If a competition charges a registration fee:

-   Cancellation requests may be considered before the registration
    deadline.
-   Refund approval depends on the specific competition rules.
-   Administrative charges may be deducted where applicable.

After the competition begins, registration fees are normally
non-refundable unless required by law.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Event Registration

If an event is cancelled by the Platform, eligible participants may
receive:

-   A full refund, or
-   Transfer of registration to a future event, where applicable.

If a participant chooses not to attend, refunds are generally not
guaranteed unless expressly stated.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Contributions & Donations

Contributions are voluntary and are intended to support the Platform.

Unless required by applicable law or approved due to an administrative
error, contributions are generally **non-refundable**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Sponsored Listings

Payments for sponsored listings (such as private hostels) may be
refunded only if:

-   The listing is rejected before publication due to reasons
    attributable to the Platform, or
-   A duplicate payment has been confirmed.

Approved and published sponsored listings are generally non-refundable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Duplicate or Incorrect Payments

Verified duplicate or accidental payments may qualify for a refund after
review.

Supporting evidence may be requested.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Refund Process

Refund Request

↓

Administrative Review

↓

Eligibility Verification

↓

Decision

↓

Refund (if approved)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Refund Timeline

Where approved, refunds will be processed within a reasonable period
using the original payment method whenever practicable.

Processing times may vary depending on banks or payment providers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. User Responsibilities

Users should:

-   Verify payment details before submission.
-   Retain payment receipts.
-   Provide accurate information when requesting a refund.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Force Majeure

Refund obligations may be affected by events beyond reasonable control,
including natural disasters, government restrictions, technical
failures, or other force majeure events.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Policy Changes

This Policy may be updated periodically.

Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the latest
version.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
