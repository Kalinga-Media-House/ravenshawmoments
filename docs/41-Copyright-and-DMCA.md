# 41 - Copyright and DMCA Policy

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\

**Effective Date:** To be announced

# 1. Purpose

This Policy explains how Ravenshaw Moments respects copyright,
trademarks, and other intellectual property rights, and how copyright
complaints are handled.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Ownership of Content

## Platform Content

Unless otherwise stated, the Ravenshaw Moments name, branding, logos,
website design, documentation, and original platform materials belong to
Ravenshaw Moments or its licensors.

## User Content

Users retain ownership of photographs, videos, articles, artwork,
certificates, and other original content they upload.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. License Granted to the Platform

By uploading content, users grant Ravenshaw Moments a non-exclusive,
worldwide, royalty-free, revocable (where reasonably possible) license
to:

-   Store
-   Display
-   Process
-   Resize and optimize
-   Archive
-   Distribute within the Platform
-   Promote the Platform using submitted content

solely for operating and improving the Platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. User Responsibilities

Users must upload only content they:

-   Own, or
-   Have permission to publish.

Users are responsible for ensuring uploaded material does not infringe
third-party rights.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Copyright Infringement

Content that infringes copyright or other intellectual property rights
may be:

-   Removed
-   Hidden
-   Restricted
-   Disabled pending review

Accounts involved in repeated infringement may be suspended or
terminated.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Copyright Complaint

A complaint should include:

-   Identification of the copyrighted work
-   Identification of the allegedly infringing content
-   Sufficient information to locate the material
-   Contact information
-   A statement of good-faith belief
-   A statement that the information is accurate and submitted by the
    rights holder or an authorized representative

------------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Counter-Notification

If content is removed in error, the affected user may submit a
counter-notification containing:

-   Identification of removed content
-   Explanation of the objection
-   Contact information
-   Good-faith statement

The Platform will review the request before restoring content where
appropriate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Repeat Infringers

Accounts repeatedly violating intellectual property rights may face:

-   Warnings
-   Upload restrictions
-   Temporary suspension
-   Permanent termination

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Trademarks

All third-party trademarks remain the property of their respective
owners.

Nothing in this Policy transfers trademark ownership.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Fair Use

Where applicable, limited use of copyrighted material for purposes such
as criticism, commentary, education, research, or reporting may be
permitted under applicable law.

Users remain responsible for ensuring such use is lawful.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Preservation of University History

Historical photographs, publications, and archival materials should be
shared responsibly and with appropriate permissions where required.

The Platform may remove archival material if valid legal concerns are
established.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Enforcement

The Platform reserves the right to:

-   Remove infringing content
-   Restrict uploads
-   Suspend accounts
-   Cooperate with lawful requests from competent authorities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 14. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal or operational
changes.
