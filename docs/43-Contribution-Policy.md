# 43 - Contribution Policy

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** Production Ready\
**Document Type:** Contribution Policy

**Effective Date:** To be announced

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Purpose

This Policy explains how voluntary contributions are received, verified,
recognized, and managed on Ravenshaw Moments.

The contribution system exists to support platform development,
preservation of university memories, community activities, and long-term
sustainability.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Guiding Principles

-   Transparency
-   Accountability
-   Voluntary participation
-   Respect for contributor privacy
-   Equal recognition
-   Secure payment verification

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Who Can Contribute?

Contributions are welcome from:

-   Current Students
-   Teachers
-   Alumni
-   Parents
-   Supporters
-   Organizations
-   Sponsors
-   Any well-wisher

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Contribution Methods

Supported methods may include:

-   UPI
-   Bank Transfer
-   Payment Gateway (future)
-   Other approved payment methods

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Contribution Workflow

Contribution Made

↓

Payment Screenshot Uploaded

↓

Profile Verification

↓

Admin Review

↓

Payment Verification

↓

Approval

↓

Recognition (if applicable)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Profile Verification

## Ravenshaw Members

The contributor may provide:

-   Roll Number
-   Department
-   Batch

If an existing profile is found, it is linked to the contribution.

If no profile exists, the user may register and complete verification.

## Non-Ravenshaw Contributors

Required information:

-   Name
-   Photograph
-   Contact Number
-   Address (optional)
-   Instagram/Profile Link (optional)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Payment Verification

Only authorized administrators may review:

-   Payment screenshots
-   Transaction details
-   Supporting information

False or manipulated payment proofs may result in account suspension.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Recognition

Approved contributors may receive:

-   Appreciation Badge
-   Appreciation Certificate
-   Public recognition (with consent)
-   Wall of Gratitude listing
-   Top Contributors of the Month eligibility

Recognition is based on verification, not solely on contribution amount.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Top Contributors of the Month

Super Admin may publish a monthly list of verified contributors.

Display may include:

-   Name
-   Profile Photo
-   Department (if applicable)
-   Contribution Month
-   Appreciation Badge

Contribution amounts are displayed only if the contributor has
explicitly consented.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Wall of Gratitude

A permanent page recognizing verified contributors.

Publication is optional and respects contributor privacy preferences.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Appreciation Certificates

The Platform may issue digital appreciation certificates after
successful verification.

Certificates may include:

-   Unique Certificate ID
-   QR Verification
-   Downloadable PDF

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Sponsorship

Organizations and businesses may sponsor:

-   Competitions
-   Events
-   Student initiatives
-   Hostel activities
-   Cultural programs

Sponsored partnerships remain subject to administrative approval.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Financial Transparency

The Platform aims to:

-   Maintain contribution records
-   Verify payments
-   Prevent fraud
-   Publish recognition responsibly

Publication of financial summaries is at the discretion of the Platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 14. Privacy

Payment screenshots and sensitive financial information remain private
and are accessible only to authorized administrators.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 15. Misuse

The Platform may reject or remove:

-   Fraudulent contributions
-   Fake payment proofs
-   Misleading sponsorship claims
-   Abuse of contributor recognition

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 16. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated periodically.
