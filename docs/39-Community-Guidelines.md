# 39 - Community Guidelines

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** Production Ready\
**Document Type:** Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** To be announced

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Purpose

Ravenshaw Moments exists to celebrate the memories, achievements,
culture, and community of Ravenshaw University. These guidelines help
maintain a welcoming, respectful, and safe environment for everyone.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Community Values

We encourage members to:

-   Be respectful.
-   Be truthful.
-   Celebrate achievements.
-   Support fellow members.
-   Preserve Ravenshaw's heritage.
-   Contribute positively.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Respect Others

Treat all users with dignity regardless of:

-   Department
-   Hostel
-   Batch
-   Gender
-   Background
-   Opinions

Healthy discussion is welcome. Personal attacks are not.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Acceptable Content

Examples include:

-   Campus memories
-   Event photos
-   Competition updates
-   Educational content
-   Alumni stories
-   Volunteer activities
-   Creative work
-   Historical archives

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Prohibited Content

Do not post:

-   Hate speech
-   Harassment or bullying
-   Threats or intimidation
-   Obscene or explicit material
-   Spam
-   Fraudulent information
-   Malware or phishing links
-   Copyright infringement
-   Illegal content
-   Impersonation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Competitions

Participants must:

-   Follow competition rules.
-   Submit original work.
-   Avoid plagiarism.
-   Respect judges' decisions.
-   Behave professionally.

Fraud may result in disqualification.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Events

Members should:

-   Respect organizers.
-   Follow venue rules.
-   Avoid disruptive behavior.
-   Cooperate during check-in.
-   Respect photography rules where applicable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Photos & Media

Upload only content that:

-   You own or have permission to share.
-   Respects the privacy of others.
-   Does not violate copyright.

Participation certificates remain private by default.

Winner certificates may appear publicly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Contributions

Contributors should:

-   Submit genuine payment proofs.
-   Avoid false claims.
-   Respect the approval process.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Reporting Problems

Users are encouraged to report:

-   Abuse
-   Spam
-   Copyright violations
-   Fake profiles
-   Offensive content
-   Security concerns

False or malicious reports may themselves violate these Guidelines.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Enforcement

Depending on severity, actions may include:

-   Content removal
-   Warning
-   Temporary restrictions
-   Account suspension
-   Permanent account removal

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Appeals

Users may request a review of moderation decisions through the official
support process.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 13. Changes

These Guidelines may be updated as the community grows.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
