# 1. Introduction

Ravenshaw Moments ("Platform", "we", "our", "us") values your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and
disclose information when you use our website and services.

By using the Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. About the Platform

Ravenshaw Moments is an independent community platform created to
preserve memories, achievements, events, competitions, and alumni
connections related to Ravenshaw University.

**It is NOT the official website of Ravenshaw University.**

Official university information should always be verified from the
official university website.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Information We Collect

## Account Information

-   Name
-   Email address
-   Password (encrypted by authentication provider)
-   Profile photograph (optional)

## Academic Information

-   Department
-   Batch
-   Roll Number (private)
-   Hostel (optional)
-   User type

## Activity Information

-   Competition registrations
-   Event registrations
-   Certificates
-   Gallery uploads
-   Contributions
-   Notifications

## Technical Information

-   Device information
-   Browser information
-   IP address
-   Log files
-   Cookies

# 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

-   Create and manage accounts
-   Verify eligibility
-   Generate certificates
-   Improve platform security
-   Deliver notifications
-   Moderate content
-   Prevent fraud and abuse
-   Improve platform functionality
-   Comply with legal obligations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Public vs Private Information

Public information may include:

-   Name
-   Public profile photo
-   Department
-   Batch
-   Public achievements
-   Winner certificates
-   Public gallery items

Private information includes:

-   Email
-   Phone number
-   Roll number
-   Date of birth
-   Participation certificates
-   Payment proofs

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Contributions

Contribution payment proofs are reviewed only by authorized
administrators.

Contributor recognition is published only after approval and according
to platform settings.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only:

-   Authentication
