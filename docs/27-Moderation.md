# 27 - Moderation

**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\
**Status:** 🔒 Frozen\
**Document Type:** Moderation Policy & Operations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

This document defines the moderation framework for Ravenshaw Moments,
ensuring community content remains respectful, authentic, lawful, and
aligned with platform policies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Objectives

-   Maintain a safe community.
-   Protect user rights.
-   Preserve historical accuracy.
-   Prevent abuse and spam.
-   Ensure transparent moderation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Moderation Scope

Moderation applies to:

-   Profiles
-   Photos
-   Videos
-   Events
-   Competitions
-   Comments (future)
-   Contributions
-   Private Hostel listings
-   Organization content
-   Department content
-   News & announcements

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Moderation Roles

## Super Admin

-   Full authority
-   Final decision on disputes
-   Policy enforcement

## Moderator

-   Review reports
-   Approve or reject content
-   Hide inappropriate content
-   Escalate complex cases

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Community Reporting

Users may report:

-   Spam
-   Harassment
-   Impersonation
-   False information
-   Copyright violations
-   Offensive content
-   Privacy violations
-   Illegal content

Every report receives a tracking status.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Moderation Workflow

Content Published

↓

User Report / Automatic Flag

↓

Moderator Review

↓

Decision

↓

Notification

↓

Appeal (if applicable)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Available Actions

Moderators may:

-   Approve
-   Reject
-   Hide
-   Restore
-   Request edits
-   Issue warning
-   Suspend temporarily
-   Escalate to Super Admin

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Appeals

Users may appeal moderation decisions.

Appeal lifecycle:

Decision

↓

Appeal Submitted

↓

Super Admin Review

↓

Final Decision

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Spam Prevention

Controls include:

-   Rate limiting
-   Duplicate detection
-   Upload limits
-   CAPTCHA (future)
-   Automated spam detection (future)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Copyright

Copyright complaints follow the Copyright & DMCA Policy.

Reported material may be temporarily hidden during review.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Transparency

Moderation actions should be:

-   Logged
-   Auditable
-   Consistent
-   Policy-based

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Audit Logs

Record:

-   Reports
-   Decisions
-   Warnings
-   Suspensions
-   Restorations
-   Appeals
