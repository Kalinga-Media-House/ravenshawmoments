**Project:** Ravenshaw Moments\
**Version:** 2.0 (Legacy)\

# Purpose

This document provides the legal foundation for Ravenshaw Moments. It
explains the platform's legal principles, governance, policy hierarchy,
user responsibilities, and relationship with Ravenshaw University.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Legal Philosophy

Ravenshaw Moments is committed to:

-   Privacy by Design
-   Transparency
-   User Trust
-   Responsible Data Handling
-   Respect for Intellectual Property
-   Compliance with applicable laws

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Platform Ownership

Ravenshaw Moments is an independent digital community platform operated
by its owners and administrators.

Unless expressly stated, it is **not owned, operated, endorsed, or
officially maintained by Ravenshaw University**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Relationship with Ravenshaw University

The platform exists to celebrate and preserve the memories,
achievements, and community life of Ravenshaw University.

Important Notice:

-   This platform is **not the official Ravenshaw University website**.
-   Official admissions, examinations, notices, and academic information
    should always be verified through the official university website.
-   If any discrepancy exists, official university information takes
    precedence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Legal Objectives

-   Protect users
-   Protect administrators
-   Protect contributors
-   Protect intellectual property
-   Ensure lawful platform operation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# User Responsibilities

Users agree to:

-   Provide accurate information
-   Respect other users
-   Follow community guidelines
-   Upload only content they have the right to share
-   Avoid illegal or abusive activity

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Platform Responsibilities

The platform will:

-   Protect user data
-   Maintain reasonable security
-   Respond to valid legal requests
-   Remove unlawful content when required
-   Publish policy updates transparently

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Data Protection

Personal data is processed according to the Privacy Policy.

Key principles:

-   Data minimization
-   Purpose limitation
-   Secure storage
-   Controlled access
-   User privacy controls

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Intellectual Property

Users retain ownership of their original content while granting the
platform a limited license to display, process, and archive approved
content according to the Terms & Conditions.

# Compliance

The platform aims to comply with applicable Indian laws governing:

-   Information Technology
-   Privacy
-   Intellectual Property
-   Electronic Records
-   Consumer Protection (where applicable)

# Dispute Resolution

Disputes should first be addressed through the platform's support
process.

Where required, applicable laws and competent courts shall govern legal
proceedings.

Material changes should be communicated through the platform.

Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of revised policies.
